Sprint Retrospective
Followed the practices for sprint retrospective meeting and curated our sprint feedback in the following format.

What we loved: 
* Completion of sprint goals.
* Good team support.
* Team communication.

What we learned:
All were new to front-end development, so we explored new things about webpage development.

What we lacked:
* Timely commits in git.
* Not able to handle dependencies effectively.

What we longed for:
* More standup meetings.

Decide what to do:
Actions to start:
* Regular commits in git.
Actions to Stop:
* Changing planning in between the sprint.
Actions to continue:
* Team communication.
* Exploring and gaining knowledge of technologies.
* Completing sprint goals.
